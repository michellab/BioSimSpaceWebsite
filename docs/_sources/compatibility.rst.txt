=============
Compatibility
=============

BioSimSpace has been tested against the following versions of its
external dependencies:


`AMBER <http://ambermd.org>`__
==============================

* AmberTools19
* AmberTools18

`GROMACS <http://www.gromacs.org>`__
====================================

* 2019 series
* 2018 series

`NAMD <https://www.ks.uiuc.edu/Research/namd>`__
================================================

* 2.13
* 2.12
